Our Mission

"We exist to help students know love and be a light to their world"- Andy Frizzell
Our youth group is designed as a place where students grades 6-12 can come and learn about God in the company of other students their age. We have several "hands on" events where students can serve God and their community. Learn more by going to our "services" page.
